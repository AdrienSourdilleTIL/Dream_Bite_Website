CLARE STENNING is a writer and actor, with a writing masters from CSSD. Her debut play #Virginie, was devised with Tom Corradini Teatro, then performed at the Teatro Rivoli, Turin. Her next play, Vacillate was part of the King’s Head Theatre’s Queer Season, 2019. She has since written several short films, including A Party to Forget, finalist at the World Monologue Film Festival.

LYDIA SABATINI is a playwright from Essex, with a writing masters from CSSD. Her work has been shortlisted for the Women’s Prize for playwriting in 2021 and longlisted in 2023. She has participated in the Bush Theatre’s Emerging Writers Group 2021/2, the Traverse Theatre’s Breakthrough Writers: In Residence Programme 2022/3, the Mercury Theatre’s Playwrights Programme 2022/3, the London Library’s Emerging Writers Programme 2023/4, Orange Tree Theatre’s Writers Collective 2023/4 and BOLD Playwrights 2024.

KATRINA BENNETT is a scriptwriter from Warwickshire, with a writing masters from CSSD. Her debut play CHOOSE YOUR FIGHTER, developed with mentorship from Barrel Organ Theatre, performed at festivals across the Midlands in 2022 before a sell- out run at Camden People’s Theatre in May 2023. She works as a Mentor Playwright for the National Theatre on their New Views programme.
